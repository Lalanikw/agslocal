ACE inhibitors are medications used for heart failure.
1. Mechanism of Action
ACE inhibitors work by blocking ACE. This is an enzyme in the body. When ACE is blocked, blood pressure goes down.
Angiotensin II is reduced. This is important for the heart.
2. Effects on Heart
The heart gets better when you take ACE inhibitors. Patients feel less tired and can do more activities.
There is less fluid in the body which is good.
3. Effects on Blood Vessels
Blood vessels relax when you take ACE inhibitors. This helps blood flow better.
Blood pressure decreases which is beneficial for heart failure.
4. Remodeling
ACE inhibitors help the heart not get worse over time. The heart structure stays better.
Studies show these medications help patients.
